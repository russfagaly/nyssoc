Where Contributions Go

The Cincinnati Gallery

Preserving and exhibiting the artifacts and documents of the Society at Mount Gulian.

Education

The Teacher of the Year award and programs that keep the Revolution's history before the public.

Charity

Assistance to members, or families of members, in need — the Society's third founding principle.
